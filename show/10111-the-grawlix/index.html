--- layout: page title: 'The Grawlix' category: show featuredimage: '/img/show-images/show-thegrawlix-300x300.jpg' --- Based in Denver, Colorado the Grawlix consists of comics Adam Cayton-Holland, Andrew Orvedhal and Ben Roy. The three of us host the show with a sketch up top - sweet, sweet banter between the three of us - and then some standup with a video or two thrown in for good measure. After much experimentation we've found that the up-front with the three of us, then a video, then pretty much a stand-up show after that (with the three of us taking turns MCing, usually with some running through-line we conceive on the fly) is the best way to pull this show off. We've done The Grawlix at Bridgetown before, as well as SXSW, The New York Comedy Festival, Riot LA and the High Plains Comedy Festival. We also just filmed a pilot for TRU TV and by the time Bridgetown rolls around we could very well have had that pilot picked up so we'll be MEGA HUGE STARTS BUT STILL WITH HUMBLE ATTITUDES AND GOOD TIME VIBES.

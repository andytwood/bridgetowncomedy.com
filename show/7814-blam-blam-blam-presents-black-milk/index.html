--- layout: page title: "Blam! Blam! Blam! Presents "Black Milk"" category: show --- Sprung from LA's 7 year-old comedy show "Blam! Blam! Blam!", "BLACK MILK" (or "Leite Preto" in Portugese) is a delicious compendium of crisp comedic performances and surprising late-night weirdness. On it's surface, here is a sturdy-walled show with a firm foundation -- solid pedigree. Inside however, it's Knucklehead City. Straight bananas. The walls will stay up and the roof won't come crashing but outside of that.... So, precisely what will go down? Exactly.

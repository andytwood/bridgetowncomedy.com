--- layout: page title: "Dr. Katz, Professional Therapist: a 20th Anniversary Celebration" category: show featuredimage: "/img/show-images/show-drkatzprofessionaltherapista20thanniversarycelebration-300x300.jpg" --- The Bridgetown Comedy Festival is proud to present a live production of Dr. Katz, Professional Therapist in celebration of the 20th anniversary of the Comedy Central debut of the groundbreaking animated program that showcased dozens of comedy legends including Conan O'Brien, Ray Romano, Louis CK, Dave Chappelle and Mitch Hedberg. For the live presentation, host Jonathan Katz welcomes special guests Janeane Garofalo (24, Mr. Show, The Ben Stiller Show), Andy Kindler (The Late Show with David Letterman, Last Comic Standing), Dana Gould (The Simpsons), Brendon Small (Metalocalypse, Home Movies) and surprise guests.

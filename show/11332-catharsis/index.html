--- layout: page title: 'CATHARSIS' category: show featuredimage: '/img/show-images/show-catharsis-300x300.jpg' --- CATHARSIS:Comics Ever Mainard, Aaron Weaver and Lindsay Adams Host CATHARSIS. Catharsis is a show that challenges comedians to reveal something they hide and release their inhibitions to the room. Audience members are encouraged to drop an anonymous confession into the "purge bucket" and free themselves of their judgements. It's a fun & humorous "safe space" with a unique experience every show.

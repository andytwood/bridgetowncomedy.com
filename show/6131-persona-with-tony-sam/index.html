A comedy show with character(s) This unique and hilarious show is full of surprises! Featuring the many faces of Tony Sam as host along with stand-up comedians performing as their funniest characters, the show has been called by Laughspin Magazine, "A total riot” and ”a stand out," at both prior sold-out appearances during the Bridgetown Comedy Festival. The Comedybureau.com says the show is "artistry in comedy at work...spectacular...fantastic", and the Portland Mercury says it is undeniably “funny”. While the show features various acts, CultureMob.com says Sam and his multiple characters in Persona! are “particularly terrific” and “some of the best parts." Come experience the unexpected at Persona!

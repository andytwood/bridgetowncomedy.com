--- layout: page title: 'Ha Fidelity' category: show featuredimage: '/img/show-images/show-hafidelity-300x300.jpg' --- Ha Fidelity is a stand up show based off your music collection from a particular time in your life (high school, college, that time your girlfriend broke up with you because she couldn't "commit"). Those songs that saved your life/you couldn't live without, and now they're (almost) too embarrassing to admit to. Stand Ups will share a sample track or two during their set to really set the mood. Let's air it out while we listen to the jams of a time gone by. Phew!

--- layout: page title: 'The K Ohle with Kurt Braunohler' category: show featuredimage: '/img/show-images/show-thekohlewithkurtbraunohler-300x300.jpg' --- A live taping of the K Ohle's GET LOST format. The entire audience will be blindfolded, put on a bus and driven somewhere fantastic they've never been. During the bus ride they'll be entertained by Kurt and a special guest comedian taunting them the whole way. Once at this VERY SECRET LOCATION, they will be un-blindfolded and allowed to explore on their own, while Kurt explains where they are, and why they're there. The entire insane experience should take about 3 hours. Prepare for walking, and possible encounters with dirt and awe.

--- layout: page title: 'Dark Spots' category: show featuredimage: '/img/show-images/show-darkspots-300x300.jpg' --- Dark Spots is a show that encourages uncomfortable subject matter chosen carefully by the performers selected for the show under a theme picked by the host and producers (Shane Torres, Alison Zeidman, and Nate Fridson). The show prides itself on conquering subjects that maybe considered taboo by many people. Dark Spots also takes a great amount of pride in having performers with different perspectives and experiences on the theme of that months show. For instance one theme was addiction and performers covered everything food addiction to gambling, to cutting. While it's a somewhat untradtional approach to a comedy show it has been something that we are very proud of and has taken on very large and dedicated crowd in here in New York.

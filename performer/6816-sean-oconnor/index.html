--- layout: page title: "Sean O'Connor" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-seano039connor-300x300.jpg" --- After starting his stand up career in New Jersey, Sean moved to New York and quickly became one of the city's favorite new comics, being featured in the NY Post as one of New York’s Top 20 Comics to Watch and filmed his Half Hour Special for Comedy Central. In addition to working as a staff writer on “Sports Show with Norm MacDonald” at Comedy Central, Sean was a producer for “Upload with Shaquille O’neal”. He is currently developing a pilot with Julian McCullough for Comedy Central.

--- layout: page title: "Bryan Cook" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-bryancook-300x300.jpg" --- Comic/writer Bryan Cook, named one of LA Weekly’s 10 Comics to Watch for 2014, is the host and creator of the live show (and podcast on the Nerdist network) Competitive Erotic Fan Fiction, one of Rolling Stone’s Top 10 Comedy Podcasts. He was featured in the comedy special Dancing Around the Shit Fire with Kyle Kinane, and performs regularly at festivals and clubs all over the country. Cook has also contributed to McSweeney’s, written for Funny or Die, Ridiculousness, Fantasy Factory, Fashion Police, JASH, Maker Studios, and various Joan Rivers projects.

He won Helium's Philly's Funniest contest in 2010 and was selected for the Oddball Festival in 2013. His show The Ministry of Secret Jokes won a Best of Philly award in 2011. He is the author of "Everything Explained Through Flowcharts" and "100 Ghosts," and has written for McSweeney's, Wired, Playboy, Fast Company, the London Times, Boing Boing, and others.

--- layout: page title: "Caroline Bassett" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-carolinebassett'-300x300.jpg" --- Caroline Bassett is a young comic from Austin, Texas who intertwines bizarre storytelling with sardonic observational jokes. She was a finalist in the 2013 Funniest Person in Austin Contest. As a downstairs neighbor once asked her, "Do you dance in the shower?"

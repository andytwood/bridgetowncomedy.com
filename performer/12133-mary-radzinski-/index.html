--- layout: page title: "Mary Radzinski " category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-maryradzinski-300x300.jpg" --- Mary is a regular feature at Helium, Goodnights and Cap City comedy clubs. Mary opened for for Jim Norton's comedy special taping at the Somerville Theater in January, 2015 as well as Rosie O'Donnell's most recent HBO comedy special at Levity Live. She was a finalist in the 2015 Boston Comedy Festival and Helium Comedy Club's 2014 "Philly's Phunniest" Contest. She was recently named Philadelphia Magazine's best comedian 2014. Mary was one of three featured comedians on CBS Channel 3's "Talk Philly", and was also featured in Philadelphia Weekly's cover story, "Funny Females of Philadelphia". She was a semi-finalist at the 2012 She-Devil Comedy Festival and performed at the 2013 Women in Comedy Festival. Mary also performed at the 2012 and 2013 Laugh Your Asheville Off Comedy Festival.

--- layout: page title: "Jackson Banks " category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-jacksonbanks'-300x300.jpg" --- A film major at the University of Utah, Jackson Banks' history is in comedy film making, and he has been doing stand up for a little under two years. He enjoys visual, surreal joke telling. He has recently participated in the Seattle International Comedy Competition, Salt Lake City's One Mic Stand, and Park City's Egyptian Theater Comedy Showcase.

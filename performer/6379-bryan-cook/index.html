--- layout: page title: "Bryan Cook" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-bryancook'-300x300.jpg" --- Bryan Cook is a writer and comedian based in LA. Cook is the host and creator of Competitive Erotic Fan Fiction, presented every month at the esteemed Nerdmelt Theater in LA, as well as all over the US. In 2012, he was featured as an opening act on Kyle Kinane’s Great Mistakes Tour, and has performed at the SF Sketchfest, Bridgetown Comedy Fest, Riot LA, and Bumbershoot Festival. Bryan is a contributor to McSweeney’s and was most recently a staff writer on E!’s “Fashion Police”.

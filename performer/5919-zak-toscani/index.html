--- layout: page title: "Zak Toscani" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-zaktoscani'-300x300.jpg" --- Zak Toscani is a Portland based comedian who performs regularly in the Pacific Northwest (Helium Comedy Club Portland, Seattle's Comedy Underground, Bumbershoot, Funny Over Everything). His style melts like an artisan cheese into both the honest and the silly, much like Dom DeLuise's lasagna recipe.

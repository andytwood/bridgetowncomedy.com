--- layout: page title: "Nick Rutherford" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-nickrutherford'-300x300.jpg" --- Nick Rutherford is a comedian hailing from Thousand Oaks, CA. He was honored to be a "Fresh Face Of Comedy" at the Just for Laughs Festival in Montreal, and was recently named one of the "Top Ten Up and Coming Comics on the West Coast" by Splitsider Magazine. Nick recently made his motion picture debut at Sundance in the feature "Toy's House" starring Nick Offerman and Megan Mullaly, and will be in the upcoming comedy "Intramural" premiering at SXSW. He has written for Adult Swim's "Loiter Squad" and currently writes at FOX for the animated shows "Axe Cop" and the upcoming "Major Lazer."

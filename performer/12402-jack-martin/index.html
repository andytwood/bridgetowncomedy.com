--- layout: page title: "jack martin" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-jackmartin-300x300.jpg" --- Jack Martin from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania currently lives in Queens, New York I've been doing stand up since 2007 started at the Laff House in Philadelphia. In 2008 I was featured on Comcast Comedy Spotlight (local). 2009 I made it to the finals of Helium Comedy Clubs Philly's Phunniest Competition (top 8 out of 150 comedians) 2010 Me and fellow comic Paul Goodman started a weekly show under the name Rittenhouse Comedy and expanded to 3 other Open Mics throughout the city, it became wildly successful a we were nominated for open mic of the year for a local Comedy award ceremony. Below are a few links to some of the press we received. http://www.urbansuburbanmagazine.com/bestkept/bestkept_rittenhouse-comed... http://citypaper.net/Blogs/TONIGHT-Rittenhouse-Comedy-Noche/ http://www.witout.net/2011/11/10/the-2011-witout-awards-best-open-mic/ 2011 & 2012 I was one of 8 organizers for the March Madness Comedy Competition which unified all the top open mics in the city and was a huge success. Type "March Madness Comedy Philadelphia" on Google, I don't want to drive you crazy with way to many links but there's plenty lol. 2014 I made the decision to move to New York its only a few hours away from Philly and alot of my friends are finding major success. It's like a training ground for some of the greats. The Clubs I've performed for in my career are: (non bringers) The Laff House, Helium, The Stress Factory, Baltimore Comedy Factory, Eastville Comedy Club, New York Comedy Club, Broadway Comedy Club & The Stand. My have many styles of jokes from observational to blue, race relations, impressions & anti-comedy. I can't tell you what "Type of Comic" I am, that's in the eye of the beholder. Thanks for the consideration and hope to hear from you soon.

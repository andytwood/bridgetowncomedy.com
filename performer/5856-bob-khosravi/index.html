--- layout: page title: "Bob Khosravi" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-bobkhosravi'-300x300.jpg" --- Bob Khosravi was born a baby, which his mother appreciated. A storyteller hailing from Austin, Texas Bob has performed all across the country as well as been a featured performer in the Moontower Comedy Festival, the Art Outside Comedy Festival, and was selected to compete in the San Francisco International Comedy Competition. An Austin favorite, Khosravi's comedy has been described as playful with a subtle sense of dark.

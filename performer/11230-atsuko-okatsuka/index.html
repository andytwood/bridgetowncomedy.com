--- layout: page title: "Atsuko Okatsuka" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-atsukookatsuka-300x300.jpg" --- Atsuko Okatsuka is a stand up comedian and filmmaker residing in Los Angeles. She is the co-founder of Dis/orient/ed Comedy, the first ever all Asian, mostly female National stand up comedy tour. She has opened for Margaret Cho at The Wiltern, performs on Anna Akana Presents: Show and Tell at the various Improv Comedy Clubs in Los Angeles, and has performed overseas in Singapore and Tokyo. You can see her on MOCATV's web series Ambiance Man where she co-stars alongside Jack Black and Fred Armisen and in award winning independent features such as Littlerock, Pearblossom Hwy, and Lake Los Angeles, available to watch on iTunes, Amazon, and Netflix.

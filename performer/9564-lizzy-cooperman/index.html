--- layout: page title: "Lizzy Cooperman" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-lizzycooperman-300x300.jpg" --- Lizzy's been featured on "The Late Late Show with Craig Ferguson," and at “New Faces” and “Dating It” at the Just For Laughs comedy festival in Montreal. She's a winner of the Laugh Across America contest and was also a finalist at the Las Vegas Comedy Festival. She's written for several shows including "The Dish," "The Eric Andre Show," "Streets of Hollywood" and "Naked & Funny." She's currently a writer for The Soup TV.

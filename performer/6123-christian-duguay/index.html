--- layout: page title: "Christian Duguay" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-christianduguay-300x300.jpg" --- Christian is an alumni of the Groundlings Theater Main Company and was a cast member on Fox's MadTV. He has also appeared on "Arrested Development" and Fox "NFL Sunday." He was featured in the Rodney Dangerfield Award showcase at the Riot LA alternative Comedy Festival in 2014, and is the Executive Producer of "Ridiculousness" (MTV) and "Fantasy Factory" (MTV). He regularly performs stand up comedy in the Los Angeles area.

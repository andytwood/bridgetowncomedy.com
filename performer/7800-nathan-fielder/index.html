--- layout: page title: "Nathan Fielder" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-nathanfielder'-300x300.jpg" --- Nathan Fielder is currently in production on the second season of his hit Comedy Central series “Nathan For You,” which he writes, directs, and stars in. Nathan has written and performed on the series “Jon Benjamin Has a Van” and “Important Things with Demetri Martin”. Hailing from Canada, Nathan is also well known for his online shorts and his weekly segments on the acclaimed CBC comedy series “This Hour Has 22 Minutes." For his work "22 Minutes" Fielder received a Canadian Comedy Award, a Writer's Guild Award, and was nominated for a Gemini.

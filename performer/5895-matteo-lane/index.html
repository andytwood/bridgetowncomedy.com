--- layout: page title: "Matteo Lane" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-matteolane-300x300.jpg" --- Matteo Lane is a New York-based comedian, originally from Chicago. Before starting stand up, Matteo lived in Italy as an oil painter and opera singer. After realizing that he can’t become Maria Callas, Matteo began telling jokes to strangers in dark rooms. Matteo has performed in the TBS Just for Laughs Festival, New York Comedy Festival, has been heard on SiriusXM and “Keith and the Girl,” and performs regularly at Caroline's on Broadway.

--- layout: page title: "Melissa Villasenor" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-melissavillasenor-300x300.jpg" --- From Los Angeles, stand-up comedian and voice impressionist Melissa Villasenor is best known from Season 6 of "America's Got Talent." She's done voices for "Adventure Time" and "Family Guy." Her four song EP, "Leap," is out on iTunes. Last year, her one person show "Melissa!" had a two month run at the Steve Allen Theater in Hollywood. It was directed and produced by comedian Ron Lynch.

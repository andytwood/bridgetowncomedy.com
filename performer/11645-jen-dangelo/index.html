--- layout: page title: "Jen D'Angelo" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-jend039angelo-300x300.jpg" --- Jen is a comedian from LA! She has appeared on Comedy Dynamic's "Coming to the Stage" for Hulu and will appear on the third season of Adam Devine's House Party on Comedy Central. She has also written for Workaholics, Cougar Town, and The Millers. Past festival credits include Just for Laughs Montreal, San Francisco Sketchfest, and the Big Sky International Comedy Competition. She also does a bunch of stuff at the UCB theater in LA. What a world!

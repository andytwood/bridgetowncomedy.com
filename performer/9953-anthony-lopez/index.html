--- layout: page title: "Anthony Lopez" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-anthonylopez-300x300.jpg" --- Anthony Lopez is a writer, comedian and a friend. He has written for the Gotham Independent Film Awards and the popular Portland based live talk show "Late Night Action". He has performed at the Bridgetown Comedy Festival, Bumbershoot, UCB East, and is a regular at several comedy clubs throughout the Northwest. He was recently a finalist in the Portland Funniest Person Contest and is a host of the popular weekly showcase, "Earthquake Hurricane."

--- layout: page title: "Adam Newman" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-adamnewman-300x300.jpg" --- Adam Newman is a New Hampshire-born, Georgia-schooled, Brooklyn-based comedian who has appeared on the Late Show with David Letterman and John Oliver's New York Stand-Up Show on Comedy Central. He was among Comedy Central's first class of "Comics to Watch" and later performed as a "New Face" at the prestigious Just for Laughs Comedy Festival in Montreal. His critically acclaimed debut stand-up CD, "Not for Horses," was released by Rooftop Comedy. Adam's TV appearances include MTV's "The CollegeHumor Show," various talking head shows on the TV Guide Network, the Tyra Banks Show (weird!), and dozens of CollegeHumor Originals on CollegeHumor.com. Adam hosts the popular "BIG LONG SETS" at NYC's Upright Citizens Brigade Theatre, and "Butt Talk," the world's #1 Number 2 podcast.

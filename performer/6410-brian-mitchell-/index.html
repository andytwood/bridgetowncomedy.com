--- layout: page title: "Brian Mitchell " category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-brianmitchell'-300x300.jpg" --- Brian Mitchell is a Jehovah’s Witness turned comedian that loves rejection and knocking on doors. He’s had the privilege of opening for TJ Miller, Louie Anderson and Josh Blue just to name a few. Brian was a finalist in the 2011 Sirius XM Opie & Anthony Car Crash Comedy contest , then in 2012 , he made the finals at the Mangers Comedy Festival, and in 2013 he received second place in Yobi.tv’s funniest sketch contest for “Token Black Guy Infomercial”.

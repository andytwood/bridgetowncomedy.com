--- layout: page title: "Karen Kilgariff" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-karenkilgariff-300x300.jpg" --- Comedian Karen Kilgariff worked as an actor on HBO's legendary sketch program Mr. Show and as the head writer for Ellen DeGeneres. But she's found a niche for herself as the composer of scathingly-funny songs about smart-phone obsession, drinking on holidays and dysfunctional relationships.

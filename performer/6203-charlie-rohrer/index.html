--- layout: page title: "Charlie Rohrer" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-charlierohrer-300x300.jpg" --- Charlie Rohrer dropped out of college to tell jokes. Comedy just seemed more viable than a bachelor's degree in Fine Arts. Growing up the black sheep of an enormous Irish family in the South Suburbs of Chicago, his comedy mostly comes from self-reflection, tales from his awkward childhood, and his unique view on everyday life. He co-producers a very successful, independent show in Chicago called "Congrats on Your Success," and has performed at Zanie’s, Chicago Underground Comedy, Gilda’s Laughfest, and more.

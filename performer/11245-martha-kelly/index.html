--- layout: page title: "Martha Kelly" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-marthakelly-300x300.jpg" --- Martha Kelly is an Austin-based comedian who has appeared on Late Night with Conan O'Brien, the Late Late Show with Craig Ferguson, and Comedy Central. She has performed at the Montreal Just for Laughs Festival, the Chicago Comedy Festival, and the Latino Laugh Festival. She plays Martha Brooks on the FX series "Baskets."

--- layout: page title: "Maggie Maye" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-maggiemaye-300x300.jpg" --- Maggie Maye has been called one of the hottest up and coming comedians in Austin, has been a multiple time finalist in the Funniest Person in Austin contest, and has been featured in Austin Monthly magazine. She also performs comedy in festivals around the country, such as the Bridgetown Comedy Festival, San Francisco Comedy Burrito Festival, and the She Devil Comedy Festival. She is also a contributing blogger for one of her favourite fests, the Women in Comedy Festival.

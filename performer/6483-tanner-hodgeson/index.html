--- layout: page title: "Tanner Hodgeson" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-tannerhodgeson-300x300.jpg" --- Tanner Hodgeson is a scared little man. He moved to Seattle from Texas three years ago to work as a software developer. He started performing comedy soon after as a way of spreading the nonsense contained inside him. He has performed in Bumbershoot Music & Arts Festival as well as local shows in the Seattle, Tacoma, and Bellingham areas including Comedy, Mystery, and Mutants! and Competitive Erotic Fan Fiction.

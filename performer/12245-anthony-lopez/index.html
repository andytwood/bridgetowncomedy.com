--- layout: page title: "Anthony Lopez" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-anthonylopez-300x300.jpg" --- Anthony Lopez co host the very success weekly stand up show Earthquake Hurricane, is the senior writer for popular Portland talk show Late Night Action and has trouble saying the letter R. He has written for the Gotham Independent Film awards, NPR's Livewire and the Willamette Week. He is often loud and has a opinions on everything. He can be seen featuring at Helium comedy club and performing at festivals and showcases across the country.

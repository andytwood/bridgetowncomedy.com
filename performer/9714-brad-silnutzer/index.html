--- layout: page title: "Brad Silnutzer" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-bradsilnutzer-300x300.jpg" --- After being “discovered” at his bus stop in the 3rd grade, Brad Silnutzer became an international superstar (read: was in a series of Ovaltine commercials, that lead everyone to make fun of his for the next 20 years). Now, ending his long hiatus from entertainment, Brad left his job as a high school teacher and became a Los Angeles-based stand up comedian. Brad performs every night at places ranging from coffee shops to The Hollywood Improv and produces some really neat LA shows: “Brew HaHa: The Comedy Show Drinkng Game”, “The Nickel Club”, “Thomas Time”, and “The Midnight Society”. People have said of his comedy, "those are some high energy stories" and "he's my son, but I don't get it" . He looks forward to eventually flaming out, and begging Ovaltine to do a retrospective commercial bringing back the boys from the past.

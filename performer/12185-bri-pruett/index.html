--- layout: page title: "Bri Pruett" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-bripruett-300x300.jpg" --- Bri Pruett is a Portland-based comedian and writer. She regularly performs at Helium comedy club, and Parlor Live! and has appeared at Bumbershoot, SF Sketchfest, Riot LA and other showcases throughout the country. She's the cohost of a monthly variety/talk show Late Night Action and weekly standup showcase Earthquake Hurricane. She's appeared on NPR's LiveWire and OPB. She's a freelance writer for the Portland Mercury and has a regular column about sex and relationships called: Let's Do It with Bri Pruett. Her cute face and epic dance moves are featured in music videos for artists such as: Bob Mould, Benji Hughes, and The Doubleclicks.

--- layout: page title: "Matt McCarthy" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-mattmccarthy-300x300.jpg" --- Matt McCarthy is a comedian, writer, improviser and actor living in Los Angeles, best known for appearances on The Pete Holmes Show, CollegeHumor.com and as host of the We Watch Wrestling Podcast, he has appeared on Louie, Curb Your Enthusiasm, Bored to Death, Just For Laughs New Faces, John Oliver's NY Stand-Up and the Adam McKay film The Other Guys.

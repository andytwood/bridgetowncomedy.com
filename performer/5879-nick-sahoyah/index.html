--- layout: page title: "Nick Sahoyah" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-nicksahoyah-300x300.jpg" --- Nick Sahoyah is a writer and comedy producer based out of Seattle, Washington. In addition to performing at and hosting comedy showcases across Seattle, Nick co-produces the popular weekly comedy night at Scratch Deli on Capitol Hill. His past production projects include organizing and emceeing the comedy stage at 'Mo Wave 2013 (Seattle's first all queer music and arts festival). Shortly after, Nick co-produced and hosted Seattle Pride Week's biggest comedy show, "Out & In" at the Neptune Theater; a benefit in partnership with Gay City Health Project. Apart from stand up, Nick produces the cult sketch/sitcom webshow "Monsoon Season" starring Jinkx Monsoon (winner of RuPaul's Drag Race Season 5), which he directs, co-writes, and co-stars in (http://www.funnyordie.com/monsoonseason), and hosts his own podcast, "That's Provocative! with Nick Sahoyah" (http://www.nicksahoyah.com). Nick has previously performed stand up at Bumbershoot Music & Arts Festival (2011/2012), 'Mo Wave (2013), Summer Meltdown Festival (2013), and Dallas Comedy Festival (2013).

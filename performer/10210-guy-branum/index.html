--- layout: page title: "Guy Branum" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-guybranum-300x300.jpg" --- Guy Branum is a stand-up comedian, actor and writer. He has guest starred on NBC's "Partners" and he was an original staff writer and panelist for Chelsea Lately, and toured with the Comedians of Chelsea Lately. He has also appeared in supporting roles in the feature films "Seeking a Friend for the End of the World" and "No Strings Attached" and has appeared on E!, MTV, G4, and Current TV. Most recently, Guy served as a staff writer and correspondent on FX's and then FXX's "Totally Biased with W Kamau Bell" and appeared on NBC's "Last Comic Standing". He also just wrote for several pilots, including Comedy Central's "Another Period" and LOGO's "Straight Out".
